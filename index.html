About Us

All In One Contracting and Renovations is a locally owned and operated business proudly serving Sault Ste. Marie, Goulais River and Algoma areas.

At All in One, we specialize in delivering high-quality construction services with a focus on craftsmanship, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction. From home renovations to large-scale commercial projects, our team is dedicated to providing reliable, timely, and professional work every step of the way.

With over 15 years of experience, we pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and our ability to bring your vision to life. Whether you’re looking to build from the ground up or update your existing space, All in One is the right choice for your next project.

We are fully insured and offer free estimates for all potential projects. Our team is fully committed to ensuring your project is done right, on time, and within budget. Contact us today to see how we can help turn your dream into a reality!
